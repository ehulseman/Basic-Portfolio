About Me

My name is Elizah Hulseman. I am 26 years old. I live in Portsmouth, NH and have since I was nine years old. When I was sixteen, I lived in Florence, Italy for a year and as a result speak fluent Italian. I love foreign languages, travel and learning about history, art, and architecture.

After graduating from the University of Edinburgh in Scotland, I moved back to Portmsouth and got a job in the software world despite having graduated with a degree in Human Geography with Environmental Studies. I have been working as a product manager for the past three and half years, working solely within the construction space. I currently live with my partner, Abner, in our duplex in Portsmouth with our two cats Agathon and Leone.
